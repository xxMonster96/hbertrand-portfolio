The Dance

These frames are the scenes I was responsible for creating on behalf of Spotted Fawn Productions.

A short animated film based on the Process Model of Eco-anxiety and Grief by Dr. Panu Pihkala, University of Helsinki. Screenplay by Elin Kelsey with Mary Beth Leatherdale and Panu Pihkala. Directed and produced by Spotted Fawn Productions. Music by Piqsiq. Warm thanks to Maj and Tor Nessling Foundation for support. For more resources and information, please see The Process of Eco-anxiety website.

To see the creation of these characters and environments, please see The Dance Concepts.
