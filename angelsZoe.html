The Younger Sister

Zoe

Was always scared of the world. All the monsters that took her dad when she was young could still be lurking around; hiding just behind the veil of the constant fog that surrounded the mall she lived in with her adoptive father. Every night she would see her dad's expression when he was yanked into the sky by a light. She would see her brother's face when the acidic ocean claimed his life.

She would relive her nightmares every night.

But, it would be her nightmares that would eventually prove to be her salvation. Forced to confront her fear of the outside world, she would soon find that her father and brother might have something to do with the monsters she feared.
