The Divine Heroes

Robin & Zoe

Two people born into a world of cruelty. For all their lives, they thought they were damned to live the lives of rats; doomed to spend their entire lives in fear of the unknown that hides behind the deadly fog that has engulfed their entire world. But, strange occurances such as lights in the sky and rumors of glowing people has made them question why they were put in this desolate world.

Are they destined to live the life of rats or were they put here for an even greater purpose?
