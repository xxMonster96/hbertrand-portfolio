The Police Station

Is a key location near the end of the Angels storyline. A desolate location that was thought to be abandoned in the middle of the city of danger and despair. Included in this page is the process of how I went about creating the piece you see.

Recieving information that one of her family members, who she thought was dead, is possibly waiting for her, Zoe decides to brave the dangers that the world she lives in poses to her to rescue them. After all, knowing that she is no longer alone in a world full of misery is enough for her to muster the courage to seek out what she's always wanted. A family.
