The World

Is a world filled with a deadly fog that shrouds all the history and mysteries of the world before the fog. A time when the air was clean, the sky was lit, and life was safer.

But, on their journeys through the world, our protagonists find signs of a sinister world before the fog. Something that makes them question whether the world before was any better than the current world.
