Queen Liarie

Is one of the central characters of Project Fire. It is her who is the mother of the lost prince, the current Queen, and the last member of the legendary royal family. As such, I felt that she would make a fitting character for me to model her in 3D since she is one of the first characters I designed for Project Fire. To see more characters from Project Fire in various states of completion, please feel free to visit Project Fire: Eiva or Project Fire: Royalty
