Project Fire

An RPG that follows the journey of a Knight and their companions as they search for a Prince who ran away from his home in search of a legendary treasure.

Set in a world filled with mysteries, magic, and wonder along with war, civil unrest, and crime, players will have a plethora of stories to find in the world of Galea.
