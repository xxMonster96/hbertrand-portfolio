Stuck in a world after utopia, where cybernetic people infected with a deadly virus wander the dead megacities of the world before, three kids must try to find a way to survive in world that no longer wants them.

Following Ariel, Baxter, and Chris, three kids looking after their comatose parents in a world of zombies, they try to find a safe haven to call their own while also figuring out the mystery behind the virus that killed the world.
