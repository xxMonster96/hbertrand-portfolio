The Fog

Keep Her Safe...

A World Bygone

Is the name of the first chapter of Angels. A story about two people separated from the ones they loved by a deadly fog that lingers in the air long after a mysterious apocalypse. If the fog wasn't bad enough by itself, there are rumours circulating in their respective communities of glowing people living in the fog and lights in the sky.

It's been said that there's always some sliver of truth behind all rumours and stories. Could this be one of those cases?
