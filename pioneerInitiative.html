Brave Explorers

To go beyond and return

The Pioneer Initiative

Is a story of a lost explorer sent to an entirely new solar system. One of the first humans to ever leave our home system, this brave explorer had become stuck in this new, strange system they now find themselves in. Lost and alone, the explorer reminiscence about their journey and what they plan to do.

But, just as they begin to accept their situation, a strange familiar face shows up that threatens to pull them back to a life they thought they escaped.
