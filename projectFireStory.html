Meeting Eiva

Is one of the pivotal moments in the Knight's journey to find the lost Prince. Eiva would go one to be a vital member of the group of Treasure Hunters who are sent to find and return the young prince back to his home.

In this section, we'll explore a little bit of Eiva's personality and how she interacts with her hero. We'll also get a glimpse into a shady criminal organization who would prove to be a nuisance to the Knight.
