The Beloved Rulers

The Royal Family

They are the rulers of the country that the game takes place in and are beloved by all of their subjects. Or, so it was until one day a royal family member decided to change the laws of ruling and almost tore the world asunder by war. Now, the surviving members must mend the damaged relationship that the Disgraced King caused and restore the love their people once had for them.
